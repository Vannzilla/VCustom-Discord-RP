VCustom Discord RP

Getting Started

  1. To get started go to the Discord Developer Portal
  2. Once there press "New Application" on the top right
  3. Set the name to what you would like to appear as playing on your status
  4. Copy your client id
  5. Paste your client id into the "Client ID" textbox on the main VCustomDRP page
  6. Fill in the other boxes to create your rich presence
  7. Press "Update RP" and see your presence!

Using Images

  1. Go to your application on the Discord Developer Portal
  2. Click on the tab that says "Rich Presence" on the left
  3. Press on the button that says "Add Image(s)"
  4. Select your image file(s)
  5. Set the name to something you can remember
  6. Enter the name in VCustomDRP in either image text boxes you choose
  7. Press "Update RP" and see your images in your presence!
